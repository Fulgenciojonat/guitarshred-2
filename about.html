About us

Meet the Team

We are passionate to teach all Music enthusiast and every individual who wants to bring their guitar playing to the next level. Our team is very passionate to bring our students to be confident and be ready to be part of the music industry. Our instructors always brings the best of their student. We are all really excited to welcome you to our team! We want all of our student to enjoy their time working with different instructor for them to enhance their technical and personal skills.

Mission Vision

The Guitar-shred guitar prepares students to understand, contribute to, and succeed in a rapidly changing Music society. We will ensure that our students develop both the skills and the passion to bring up the confidence in working on music. We will also lead in generating practical and theoretical knowledge that enables people to better understand our world and improve conditions for local and global communities.
